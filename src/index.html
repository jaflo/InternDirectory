FAQ

Questions you might have.

Why isn't the directory up yet?

I just want to make sure that the directory has enough entries to be worth sharing and that it doesn't have any blatant errors. This takes some time.

Who is this for?

Mainly CS students at the University of Texas at Austin that want to help or get help from their fellow students.

Why can't I join the directory?

Did you sign in with your UT Google account? You need to have access to an @utexas.edu email address to join.

Why can't I view the directory?

Did you sign in with your UT Google account? Have you filled out the survey? You need to join the directory before you can view it.

How can I edit my response?

Head over to this form and follow the link to edit there.

How can I remove my information?

Let me know through this form and I'll try to get to it ASAP.

I need to complain to someone.

Not a question, but please don't be angry. You can send me an email if you want.
